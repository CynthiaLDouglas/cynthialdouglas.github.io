About Me...

Hello. Call me Cynthia. I’m a software engineer ready to make a change in this world. There are so many facets of life being touched by technology and yet so many people being left behind as this industry continues to grow and shape our world. Shouldn’t we all be able to access it? I am on a mission. You can join me if you’d like to increase the accessibility of technology to everyone. Be it a website that needs support in rebuilding its content to be accessible to all or a product that you would like to share with more than your current base. I’m here to support you in making it possible. Let’s first share this moment but then join in me in the movement toward progress.
